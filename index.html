Client Scenarios

Not sure if you need IT controls support? Here are a few common situations where clients bring me in:

  • SOX Readiness: You’re under pressure to establish a SOX-compliant control environment, but aren’t sure where to start or how to prioritize.
  • Post-audit remediation: You've just been through an external audit and need help addressing IT control deficiencies or gaps.
  • Building from scratch: Your company is growing fast, and you need to implement IT controls and governance to stay ahead of future requirements.
  • Maturing existing controls: Your current controls are too manual, too broad, or inconsistently applied — and you'd like a more sustainable approach.
  • Ongoing advisory: You need a trusted advisor to support internal teams with risk assessments, role design, or periodic reviews as your environment evolves.
“When controls are designed effectively from the beginning, everything downstream — from operations to audits and testing — becomes far more efficient and manageable.”
